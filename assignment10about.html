Who is Joey Rossi?

That guy to your right? That's Joey Rossi.

Joey Rossi is a student at Illinois State University with a major in Creative Technologies (CTK) and a minor in Communication Sciences and Disorders (CSD). Creatively, Rossi is most familiar with audio and music but the CTK curriculum has encouraged him to step out of his comfort zone into visual mediums.

Rossi's biggest inspirations also happen to be his favorite bands. These include, The Beach Boys, The Police, Sparks, and The Smashing Pumpkins (obviously).

Joey is available on Twitter, Instagram and Facebook (links at bottom of every page) however the best method of contact is email.

Joey Rossi's Email: jfross1@ilstu.edu
